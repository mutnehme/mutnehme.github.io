Some thoughts on Theology

Why theology?

Theology shapes the laws and the mores of people. They have their traditions, their beliefs, their virtues, their vices. I find it fascinating to know these laws and mores and to study them in detail and in context. I like to study what the Enlightenment era thinkers have to say as well as studying the history and the contexts in which religions have lived.

For many religions this has a benefit of knowing their predecessors. Islam views Christinity as a prior virtuous religion. Christianity views Judaism as their prior religion.

Likewise they all interact with each other and create societies. America is the epitome of this, having many of the religions of the world contained in one polity.
